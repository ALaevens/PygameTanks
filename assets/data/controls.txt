Controls for Tanks:
As for now the tanks are keyboard controlled. Both players use the same keyboard to play.

The controls have three groups:
    Movement, Relative Aim, and Fire

Player 1:
    Movement: W, A, S, D
    Relative Aim: Q, E
    Fire: F

Player 2:
    Movement: I, J, K, L
    Relative Aim: U, O
    Fire: H

You can quit the game at any time by pressing ESC

You can skip any 'level' by pressing Right Arrow
